Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

Dopfast ("we", "our", "the app") is a digital wellbeing application developed by Solenos Yazılım Teknoloji. Your privacy is fundamental to our mission. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Dopfast is designed with a privacy-first, on-device architecture. We collect as little data as possible.

On-Device Only (never leaves your phone): Screen time data, app usage statistics, pickup counts, Quiet Time schedules, Balance Score, streak data, and all personal metrics are processed and stored entirely on your device using Apple's DeviceActivityFramework and local storage. We never have access to this data.

AI Coach messages: When you use the AI Coach feature, your messages are sent to our secure server (Cloudflare Worker) which forwards them to an AI language model (Anthropic Claude) to generate responses. Messages are not stored on our servers after the response is generated. We do not use your messages to train AI models.

Anonymous device identifier: A randomly generated device ID is sent with AI Coach requests for rate limiting purposes only. This ID cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, browsing history, or any other personal information. We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or third-party data collection tools.

3. Screen Time Data

Dopfast uses Apple's Screen Time APIs (FamilyControls, DeviceActivity, ManagedSettings) to monitor and manage your screen time. All screen time data is processed on your device by Apple's frameworks. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

4. Subscriptions & Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

5. Data Storage & Security

All personal data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms (SwiftData, UserDefaults). AI Coach messages are transmitted via HTTPS encryption. Our Cloudflare Worker proxy does not log or store message content.

6. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Anthropic's Claude API for the AI Coach feature. Anthropic's data handling practices can be found at anthropic.com/privacy. No other third-party services receive your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Dopfast is rated 9+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information to function.

8. Your Rights

Since your data is stored on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the Dopfast app from your device. There is no account to delete and no server-side data to request removal of.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@dopfast.ai
